I'm bullish on agentic commerce, but when Google released the UCP standard there was no easy way to simulate how it actually worked. So I built one. Tell Gemini what you want to buy and watch it autonomously discover retailers, build a cart, apply discounts, and complete the transaction, no browsing or clicking required.
