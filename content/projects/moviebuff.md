Your movie taste deserves more than a list. MovieBuff turns film discovery into an infinite, explorable canvas — search for any movie and watch it land in a living genre map you can pan, zoom, and build on. When you're ready for what's next, hit Generate and let AI surface personalized picks based on what's already on your board.
