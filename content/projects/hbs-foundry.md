HBS Foundry is a new AI venture at Harvard Business School building an AI platform for deep-tech founders who are turning scientific breakthroughs into fundable companies. At Foundry I lead a cross-functional team to take Foundry from blank page to a launched product serving founders in all 50 U.S. states.

The platform runs on an agentic core: 10+ AI agents we shipped to compress the early founder workflow — idea diagnosis, problem framing, pitch coaching, competitive analysis, lead generation — into a guided, multi-step experience that combines learning, productivity, and accountability.
